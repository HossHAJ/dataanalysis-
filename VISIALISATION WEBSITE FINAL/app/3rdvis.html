Bubble chart of the fuel consumption by each car's brand

This bubble chart visualizes fuel consumption (in L/100 km) by car brand. Each bubble represents a car brand. The size of the bubble corresponds to the quantity of the fuel consumption. Clicking on a bubble reveals the exact fuel consumption value. Brands like Jeep, SsangYong and Ferrari have the largest bubbles, meaning their cars tend to have higher average fuel consumption. On the contrary, brands like Smart, Bentley and Mini have smaller bubbles. This graph highlights which brands focus more on fuel-efficient cars and which ones lean towards high-consumption models. NB: There are some brands like Rolls-Royce, Suzuki, Hyundai and Lada that do not appear in bubbles. It is because their fuel consumption is too low compared to the other brands, so the bubbles would be too small to appear.
