Scatter Plot of the CO2 emissions in relation to the car weight

Thanks to this plot, we can notice many different things. Indeed, first, we can see that if we ignore some exceptions, the plot follows a linear function; the heavier the car, the more power it has to be, the more it pollutes... On the left of the plot, there are all the small cars such as Lada, Mini coopers, Fiat 500 etc.... and on the right side, there are all the vans, Rolls Royce, 4 by 4 etc... But there are some exceptions: sports'cars. Indeed, sports'cars need to be as light as possible, and as powerful as possible. That's why we have big values in the center left of the plot.
