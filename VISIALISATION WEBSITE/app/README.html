Dataset Analysis

Today, pollution from the transport sector accounts for 60.6% of total pollution in Europe. Many people wonder if they should buy a car that minimizes emissions. This dataset allows us to explore different factors affecting vehicle pollution and identify the most environmentally friendly options.

Description of the Data

  • What is the data?

This dataset contains information about cars, including CO‚ÇÇ emissions, horsepower, hybrid status, and more.

  • Where does it come from?

The dataset is sourced from the official French government website: Link to dataset

  • Data Provenance

The data is provided by the French government and includes detailed vehicle specifications.

  • Census or Sample?

It is a sample dataset containing data on various car models.

  • Raw or Processed?

The data has been processed to remove unnecessary columns, such as administrative model names and timestamps, ensuring a cleaner dataset for analysis.

Description of Variables

  1. lib_mrc - The brand name of the car (categorical).
  2. lib_mod - The model name of the car (categorical).
  3. cnit - The National Type Identification Code (categorical).
  4. cod_cbr - Fuel type code (e.g., 'ES' for petrol, 'GO' for diesel) (categorical).
  5. hybride - Indicates whether the car is hybrid ('yes' or 'no') (categorical).
  6. puissadmin - Administrative horsepower (discrete, range: 1-81 HP).
  7. puiss_max - Maximum power of the car (discrete, range: 10-560 HP).
  8. typ_boite_nb_rapp - Gearbox type (e.g., 'M' for manual, followed by the number of gears) (categorical).
  9. conso_mixte - Average fuel consumption in L/100 km (continuous, range: 0.6-24.5 L/100 km).
  10. CO‚ÇÇ - CO‚ÇÇ emissions in g/km (continuous, range: 13-572 g/km).
  11. masse_ordma_max - Vehicle weight with a full tank and accessories (continuous, range: 825-3094 kg).
